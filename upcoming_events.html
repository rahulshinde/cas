The Center for Afrofuturist Studies (CAS) is an artist residency program that reimagines the futures of marginalized peoples by generating dynamic working spaces for artists of color. Through community-based and public programs-focused residencies, the CAS produces workshops, exhibitions, screenings, and other public engagements in conjunction with visiting artists.
